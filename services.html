Our Services

Superior Medicine provides comprehensive concierge medical services in the comfort of your home. We specialize in personalized care for patients of all ages.

Home Visit Services

Sick Visits

Assessment of mild to moderate illnesses in your home and treatment for conditions such as strep throat, mono, RSV, influenza, covid, various upper respiratory infections, urinary tract infections, and ear infections. Prescriptions can be sent to the pharmacy of your choice.

Chronic and Primary Care Visits

Completion of work or sports physical (excluding DOT physicals) in your home. Order simple routine labs and/or some diagnostics. Bridge prescriptions for routine medications. Blood pressure, diabetes and obesity screenings as well as other health assessment screenings.

Acute Care/Injury Visits

Assessment of minor injuries in your home. Injuries such as sprains, strains, minor wound care and repair. *Limited suturing. Can order diagnostic x-rays at some outpatient radiology locations.

Additional Services

Point of Care Testing

Various diagnostic tests available at your location. Additional testing is billed separately based on specific requirements.

Bridge Prescriptions

Short-term prescription assistance when you can't get to your regular doctor or need medication management between appointments.

Health Consultations

Professional medical advice and guidance for health concerns, medication questions, or general wellness planning.
