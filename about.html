About Lora Shima, FNP-C

"Your health is my priority, providing expert guidance in the comfort of your home."

Professional Background

I've been working in healthcare since 1998, bringing over 25 years of experience to Superior Medicine. As both a healthcare professional and a mother who has lived and raised a family in Superior, Colorado for over 25 years, I understand the unique healthcare needs of our community.

My journey in healthcare began with a strong foundation in science. Originally from Florida, I completed a Bachelor's degree in Microbiology from the University of Florida. Seeking to make a more direct impact on patient care, I moved to Texas to pursue a Bachelor's in Nursing from Texas Women's University.

Clinical Experience

Throughout my career, I have worked in a diverse range of healthcare settings, gaining valuable experience that benefits my patients today:

Medical/Surgical Nursing

Comprehensive care for patients with acute and chronic medical conditions requiring surgical intervention and complex medical management.

Oncology/Hematology

Specialized care for patients battling cancer and blood disorders, providing compassionate support during challenging times.

Mom/Baby Care

Caring for mothers and newborns during the precious early days of life, ensuring healthy starts for families.

Pediatrics

Specialized care for children from birth through adolescence, understanding the unique needs of growing families.

Advanced Practice Education

To expand my ability to serve patients, I pursued advanced education in family medicine. I graduated with a Master's in Nursing with a focus in Family Medicine from Regis University in 2022. This advanced training allows me to provide comprehensive primary care services including diagnosis, treatment, and ongoing health management.

For the past 3 years, I have been working in both primary care and urgent care settings with a special focus on preventive medicine - helping patients maintain their health and catch potential issues early.

Why Concierge Medicine?

Having experienced the healthcare system both as a provider and as a parent, I understand the frustrations of long wait times, rushed appointments, and difficulty accessing care when you need it most. Superior Medicine was born from a desire to provide the kind of personalized, accessible healthcare that every family deserves.

By bringing medical care directly to your home, I can spend the time needed to truly understand your health concerns, provide thorough assessments, and develop treatment plans that work for your lifestyle and schedule.
