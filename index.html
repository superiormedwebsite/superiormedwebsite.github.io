Welcome

Experience the convenience of local concierge medical services right at your doorstep! As a caring nurse practitioner and a mom with over 25 years of living and raising a family in Superior, Colorado, I understand urgent care needs of families. Whether you're in need of a quick sports physical for your child or help diagnosing a fever on a Sunday evening, I'm here to provide quality medical care with compassion and the nurturing touch of a mom.

I have experiences with all ages birth to 100 and have worked in both urgent care and family practice settings. At this time I am only accepting cash payments for services (cash, check or Venmo). Sick home visits and sports physicals starting at $75.00. Any additional point of care testing is billed separately.

I've been working in health care since 1998. Originally from Florida, I completed a Bachelor's in Microbiology from the University of Florida and later moved to Texas to complete a bachelor's in Nursing from Texas Women's University. I have worked in a large variety of fields including medical/surgical, oncology/hematology, mom/baby, and pediatrics. I graduated with a Master's in Nursing with a focus in family medicine from Regis University in 2022. I have been working in both the primary care and urgent care settings with a focus on prevention for the past 3 years.

My website is under construction as I learn how to build it! Thanks for your patience and I look forward to serving you and your family soon!
